Variety of Animals

There are so many animals across the world.

Sky

There are animals that could exist in the sky and fly across the sky. Some of the animals are hawks, eagles, sparrows, and much more. Additionally, all of these animals differ by their height, size, the size of their beaks, and wings.

Ground

There are many animals that live on land. Some of them are made to be cared for by humans, such as dogs and cats. There are also wildlife animals such as:

  • elephants
  • giraffes
  • snakes
  • bears

Water

The last category I'm going to discuss about the animals is the ocean life. Sharks, whales, fish, and much more ocean animals make up the ocean life.
